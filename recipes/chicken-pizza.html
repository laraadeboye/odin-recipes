Chicken Pizza

A Slice of Flavorful Perfection!

Chicken pizza is a popular variation of traditional pizza that includes chicken as one of its primary toppings. It's a versatile and delicious dish that can be customized with various sauces, cheeses, and additional toppings to suit your taste.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 1 pound (450 grams) pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Flour (for dusting)

For the Pizza Toppings:

  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken breast (seasoned to taste)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce or barbecue sauce (or a combination of both)
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers (red, green, or any color you prefer)
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves or parsley for garnish (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for added spice)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven as it preheats.

  2. Dust your work surface with flour and roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness. You can make one large pizza or divide the dough to make multiple smaller ones.

  3. Place the rolled-out dough onto a pizza stone, baking sheet, or pizza pan lined with parchment paper. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  4. Sprinkle about half of the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Then, distribute the shredded chicken, sliced red onion, sliced bell peppers, and black olives (if using) over the pizza.

  5. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese on top of the toppings.

  6. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven. Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two. Garnish with fresh basil leaves or parsley and, if you like it spicy, sprinkle some red pepper flakes over the top. Slice the pizza and serve immediately.

    8. Chicken pizza is best enjoyed fresh out of the oven while the cheese is still hot and gooey.
